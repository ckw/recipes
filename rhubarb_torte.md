# Rhubarb Torte

## Crust:
- 1 c. Flour
- 1/3 c. powdered sugar
- 1/2 c. softened butter

## Filling:
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 c. sugar
- 1/4 c. Flour
- 1/4 t. salt
- 1 T. Vanilla
- 2 1/2 - 3 c. chopped rhubarb, about 5 stalks = 3 c.

Pour over hot crust and bake for 30 min.

**Note:** If using frozen rhubarb, increase flour in filling to 1/2 c.
